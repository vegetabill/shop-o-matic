Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Shop-o-matic ("we", "our", or "us") is a household shopping list app. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Google.
  • Shopping data — the items, stores, and categories you create within your household.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the app: syncing your shopping list across household members and remembering past items for autocomplete. We do not use your data for advertising or analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Household members you have invited can view and edit the shared shopping list.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.
